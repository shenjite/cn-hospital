PUMCH

Union Medical College

Grade A Class 3 hospital

Peking Union Medical College Hospital (PUMCH) is a top-tier comprehensive hospital in China, with a history dating back over a century. It integrates medical treatment, teaching, and scientific research, and is renowned for its expertise in tackling complex and difficult diseases. PUMCH houses a large team of senior medical experts and advanced medical equipment, providing high-quality healthcare services to both domestic and international patients. It also plays a leading role in medical education and clinical research, contributing significantly to the development of China’s medical industry.

The First Affiliated Hospital of Sun Yat-sen University

Zhongshan Medical College

Grade A Class 3 hospital

The First Affiliated Hospital of Sun Yat-sen University (FAH-SYSU), also known as "Zhongshan First Hospital", is a large 3A hospital with its headquarters located in Yuexiu District of Guangzhou. Founded in 1910, it is one of the oldest and most well-known comprehensive hospitals in South China. The hospital is affiliated to Sun Yat-sen University and is directly managed by the National Health Commission. It is well known for its excellent service in China and Southeast Asia.The headquarters, also called Yuexiu Division, is located at No. 58, Zhongshan 2nd Road, Yuexiu District, Guangzhou.

The Third People's Hospital of Shenzhen

Jinan University

Grade A Class 3 hospital

Shenzhen People's Hospital (also known as the Second Clinical Medical College of Jinan University and the First Affiliated Hospital of Southern University of Science and Technology). Founded in 1946, it is a public hospital in Shenzhen and a national Grade A tertiary hospital. It was renamed in 1979. It serves as a non-affiliated hospital of Jinan University and a directly affiliated hospital of Southern University of Science and Technology. It currently has four campuses: the First Outpatient Department, the Inpatient Department and the Second Outpatient Department , the Longhua Branch, and the Bantian Campus.

The Third People's Hospital of Shenzhen

Southern University of Science and Technology

Grade A Class 3 hospital

Shenzhen Third People's Hospital (a Class A tertiary research hospital administered by the Shenzhen Municipal People's Government and a designated Shenzhen Medical Insurance hospital) is located at 29 Bujibulan Road, Longgang District, Shenzhen. The current president is Lu Hongzhou. In 1985, Shenzhen Third People's Hospital's predecessor, Shenzhen Donghu Hospital, opened. In 1996, the Shenzhen Institute of Liver Diseases was established. In 2000, the hospital's renovation and expansion project was approved. In February 2003, it became the designated hospital for the treatment of SARS in Shenzhen. In January 2004, it was renamed "Shenzhen Third People's Hospital" and relocated to 29 Bulan Road, Longgang District. In 2008, the hospital was designated the Shenzhen Branch Laboratory of the State Key Laboratory of Emerging Infectious Diseases of the University of Hong Kong.

Shanghai Renji Hospital

Shanghai Jiao Tong University

Grade A Class 3 hospital

Renji Hospital, founded in 1844, was the first Western medicine hospital established in Shanghai after the opening of the port. Currently comprised of four campuses (East, West, South, and North) and the Shanghai Cancer Research Institute, the hospital is a comprehensive, Class-A tertiary hospital offering a comprehensive range of medical services, teaching, and research. The hospital has been awarded the title of National Civilized Unit for four consecutive years and has received an A grade in the annual performance appraisal of the president of the Shanghai Shenkang Hospital Development Center for 18 consecutive years. The East Campus, located in the Lujiazui Financial and Trade Zone in Pudong, opened in October 1999. The West Campus, located in the Huangpu Central Business District, is adjacent to the Bund. The South Campus, located in Pujiang Town, Minhang District, opened in December 2012 .

Guangdong Provincial People's Hospital

Guangdong Academy of Medical Sciences

Grade A Class 3 hospital

Guangdong Provincial People's Hospital is a large, modern, Class-A tertiary comprehensive hospital integrating medical care, teaching, and research. Its medical services are based in South China and radiate throughout the country, with key medical indicators ranking among the best in the country. It currently has 13 national key clinical specialties and 32 Guangdong Provincial key clinical medicine specialties, specializing in the multidisciplinary diagnosis and treatment of difficult and critical illnesses. In the five years ending in 2023, it has introduced 326 new medical technologies, completed the registration of 9 national and 62 provincial restricted clinical application technologies, including artificial intelligence-assisted treatment technology, and completed 35.78% of Level 4 surgeries. It currently has six outpatient clinics, six research institutes, six medical centers, and the Guangdong Bone Marrow Bank, among other clinical, teaching, and research institutions.
